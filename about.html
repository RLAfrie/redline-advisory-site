About the founder

Obsessed since before the front seat.

JACK — FOUNDER, REDLINE ADVISORY

A FRIEND'S McLAREN 600LT — THE EDUCATION OF RIDING SHOTGUN

The obsession came first. Before I could see over a dashboard I could name everything that passed us on the highway, and two decades of studying this world since have only sharpened that instinct into a discipline.

The craft got its education in other people's garages. A Pagani owner once let me drop into his Huayra and fire it up; today, my closest friends own the cars I grew up memorizing. Years alongside real ownership taught me how this market actually works — how these cars are optioned, bought, maintained, and sold, and exactly where buyers get it wrong.

When it came time to buy my own first serious sports car, I approached it the way I approach everything: methodically. Months of spec research, market tracking, and disciplined comparison of every comparable car in the country — work that led me to my BMW M4. I enjoyed every hour of it. Most buyers don't have those hours, or don't want them. Redline Advisory is that same process, run for you.

By day I work on the institutional side of one of the largest financial custodians, serving registered investment advisors — work that trained me to treat numbers with discipline and risk with respect. I'm not a licensed financial advisor, and nothing here is financial advice. What I sell is market intelligence on the cars, and a straight answer about which one fits your life.
