How the package runs

Four steps. One right answer.

STEP 01

Intake

A structured questionnaire on how you'll actually use the car, what you value, and your real numbers. Thirty minutes of your time, once.

STEP 02

Research & market scan

I map your profile against the market: model families, the years and specs that matter, known issues, and current asking prices versus sold results.

STEP 03

The report

Three specific cars — live listings you could pursue this week — each with the case for it, the watch-outs, and where the price sits against the market.

STEP 04

Review call

Thirty minutes together on the report: questions, trade-offs, and your plan for next steps — inspections, financing conversations, and timing.

Straight scope

What I don't do.

I don't inspect cars in person, coordinate pre-purchase inspections, or negotiate on your behalf — and I won't pretend to until I've earned it. You'll walk into every conversation knowing more than the person across the table expects you to.
